I build production systems at the intersection of **commerce, FinTech, and agentic AI** — from WhatsApp social-selling bots wired to headless APIs, to national KPI platforms and FinTech underwriting portals.

**11+ years** shipping for **Rich Cookies**, **Revenued**, **Adobe Experience League**, **END. Clothing**, **Kitchen Warehouse**, **Viu OTT**, and **Qinsights Qatar**. Microsoft Certified Azure Developer Associate (2024). Previously at Viu · ValueLabs · Oracle · Infosys.

**Currently:** agentic commerce · 48-hour MVPs for founders & enterprise pilots · open to freelance · C2H · C2C worldwide
